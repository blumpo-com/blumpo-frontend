## **1. The "Race to the Bottom" in B2B Creative**

### **1.2. Ignoring the "Messy Middle"**

Most instant AI tools are good at two things: catchy top-of-funnel hooks and aggressive bottom-of-funnel "buy now" CTAs.
They fail miserably at the "messy middle" of the B2B journeyâ€”the evaluation phase where buyers need educational content, comparison data, and complex objection handling.

### **1.3. Lack of Strategic Anchoring**
