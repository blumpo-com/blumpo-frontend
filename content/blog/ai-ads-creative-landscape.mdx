## **Introduction**

Advertising is undergoing its most disruptive shift in decades.

With rising competition, fragmented attention, privacy restrictions and diminishing targeting precision, marketers can no longer rely on old methods. Creative is now the single biggest performance lever, influencing more than 70% of campaign results across major ad platforms.

The challenge?

Modern advertising requires:

- higher creative volume
- constant experimentation
- varied messaging
- adaptive formats
- real-time insight into customer needs

Traditional workflows simply cannot keep up.

This is why AI has become essential, not as a shortcut but as the new operating system for high-performance advertising.

In this article, we explore how AI is reshaping the entire ads ecosystem in 2025.

## **1. Why AI Is Now Essential in Modern Advertising**

### **1.1. The Explosion of Creative Demand**

Algorithms reward volume, variety and freshness.

Marketers now need:

- more hooks
- more angles
- more visuals
- more formats
- more audience-specific variations

AI makes this scale possible.

### **1.2. Declining Targeting Accuracy**

After iOS 14.5 and the decline of third-party data:

- audience precision dropped
- lookalikes weakened
- attribution became harder

Creative relevance became the new targeting.

AI helps analyze patterns and align messaging with emerging intent signals.

### **1.3. Acceleration of Market Cycles**

Trends, product categories and competitive landscapes shift weekly.

Marketing teams must:

- react faster
- iterate daily
- launch more experiments
- refresh creatives constantly

AI enables this agility.
